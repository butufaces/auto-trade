## 🔑 IMPORTANT TIPS

### ✅ DO
- ✅ **Read package details** before investing
- ✅ **Start small** if new to investing
- ✅ **Save multiple wallets** (different cryptos)
- ✅ **Verify email ASAP** after signup
- ✅ **Keep your seed phrase safe** (wallet security)
- ✅ **Contact support** if any questions
- ✅ **Check live growth** to motivate yourself
- ✅ **Diversify** across multiple packages

### ❌ DON'T
- ❌ **Don't refresh payment page** (it will cancel)
- ❌ **Don't send more than requested** amount
- ❌ **Don't share your wallet seed phrase**
- ❌ **Don't invest more than you can afford** to lock up
- ❌ **Don't send to wrong wallet address**
- ❌ **Don't miss email verification** (withdrawal will fail)
- ❌ **Don't use old wallets** from exchanges
- ❌ **Don't panic on red days** (prices fall, ROI stays same)

## ❓ COMMON QUESTIONS

**Q: How long before my investment matures?**
A: Duration depends on package. Starter = 30 days, Growth = 60 days, Premium = 90 days. Starts when payment is approved.

**Q: Can I withdraw before maturity?**
A: Yes! You can withdraw daily profit (20% of accrual) anytime. Full amount available only at maturity.

**Q: What if I send too much?**
A: Admin will see the error. Contact support with transaction ID. They'll process refund.

**Q: How do I know my wallet address is correct?**
A: Copy directly from your wallet app. Don't type it manually. Admin will verify before sending payment.

**Q: What if crypto price crashes?**
A: Doesn't affect your ROI. You get fixed 10-18% regardless of market price.

**Q: Can I have multiple investments at same time?**
A: Yes! You can have up to 10 concurrent investments. Good for diversification.

**Q: How do I know when admin approves?**
A: Bot sends notification immediately. Also shows in your portfolio as "ACTIVE" status.

**Q: What payment methods are available?**
A: Crypto (BTC, ETH, USDT, LTC) instant approval. Bank transfer takes 2-3 business days.

**Q: Is it safe?**
A: Yes. Multi-step verification, email confirmation, admin approval, and blockchain confirmation all protect your funds.

**Q: Can I withdraw profit multiple times before maturity?**
A: Yes! Each day adds more profit to the 20% withdrawable portion. Withdraw as much as you want.

**Q: What happens to my locked profit (80%)?**
A: Stays in your investment, compounds, and increases your portfolio value. Yours to withdraw at maturity.
